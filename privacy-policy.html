Privacy Policy for Asteroffit

Effective Date: 18.08.2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information (such as name, email, location, or contacts) in the current version of the game. The game does not collect device identifiers or transmit personal data to external servers.

In-game persistent data: We store your in-game progress data locally on your device. This includes your current coin balance and any other gameplay progress that must persist between sessions.

2. Use of Information

The locally stored in-game data (for example, coin balance and progress) is used solely to restore your progress when you relaunch the game and to enable gameplay features such as shooting, boss interactions, and the in-game shop. This information is not shared with third parties and is not used for advertising or analytics in the current version.
